Employment Legal Agents

Worker classification · FLSA compliance · handbook audit

1

Policy Ingestion

Paste an employee handbook excerpt, contractor classification scenario, or policy clause. The system parses jurisdictional context and identifies relevant legal frameworks.

↓ classified by jurisdiction & legal framework ↓
2

Classification Analysis

Two-axis risk scoring: contractor misclassification risk (IRS 20-factor, ABC test, economic realities) and exemption classification risk (FLSA duties test, salary basis). Each axis returns a 0–100 score with reasoning.

↓ risk scores → compliance dimensions ↓
3

Compliance Audit

Multi-dimensional review across wage & hour, leave policies, discrimination, reasonable accommodation, and recordkeeping. Each dimension scored 0–100 with risk level (low/medium/high) and narrative reasoning.

↓ compliance scores → recommendations ↓
4

Recommendation Engine

Prioritized action items based on risk severity — including policy revision flags, reclassification urgency, and jurisdiction-specific remediation steps. Every recommendation traceable to its source dimension.

↓ audit trail ↓
5

Governance Artifacts

Full audit trail captured: prompt, model version, scores, chain of reasoning, and any human overrides. Auditor-ready output with immutable timestamps.
