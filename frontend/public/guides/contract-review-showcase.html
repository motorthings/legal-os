How It Works

1
Intelligent Routing
Contract arrives → System classifies type (Vendor, Customer, Employment, DPA, General) with confidence scoring. Admits uncertainty rather than forcing classification.
2
Multi-Agent Analysis
Contract-type-specific agent deployed. Extracts parties, dates, key terms, obligations. Identifies red flags (critical), yellow flags (moderate concern), and novel clauses. Every flag includes a recommendation.
3
Weighted Risk Scoring
0–100 risk score across 5 weighted dimensions: Financial Exposure (25%), Compliance & Legal (25%), Operational Risk (20%), Ambiguity & Clarity (15%), Term Favorability (15%).
4
Role-Optimized Views
Same analysis, 4 stakeholder perspectives: Legal (risk + clauses), Operations (business impact + SLAs), Executive (portfolio trends + outliers), Junior Staff (guided learning + context).
5
Continuous Improvement Loop
Reviewer feedback → pattern extraction (weekly/monthly) → prompt refinement → accuracy tracking. Institutional knowledge compounds rather than walking out the door.
