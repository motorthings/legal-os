FAQ

10 Questions Every Lawyer Asks

  • "Will this replace me?" — No. AI handles first-pass volume work so you focus on judgment.
  • "Is my client data safe?" — In firm tools, yes (ZDR). In consumer tools, never.
  • "How do I know it's not making things up?" — Chain of reasoning is visible. You verify the source.
  • "How do I bill for AI-assisted work?" — Bill actual review time, not saved time. ABA 512.
  • "Can clients opt out?" — Yes, per the firm's published AI governance framework.
  • "What's the difference between Harvey and ChatGPT?" — ZDR, multi-model, legal-specific, auditable vs. none of those.
  • "I already use ChatGPT. Why switch?" — Confidentiality, auditability, quality.
  • "What if the AI gets it wrong and I don't catch it?" — Low-confidence outputs escalate automatically.
  • "How do I get started?" — 45-min workshop, one real task, 5-min review, report feedback.
  • "My work is too bespoke for AI." — AI does the first pass. You still do the bespoke part.
