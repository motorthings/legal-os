## Slide 3: How We Use AI (Not How We Talk About It)
- **Contract review**: 5 specialized AI agents analyze contracts against 30+ legal standards. 360 contracts/hour throughput. Attorney reviews every flag before it reaches you.
- **Due diligence**: AI ingests hundreds of documents, compares every clause against deal-specific standards, surfaces only deviations. Compresses 15-20 hours of manual review to ~2 hours of targeted human review.
- **Matter intake**: Every new engagement evaluated across 5 dimensions in under 10 seconds. Conflicts flagged before the engagement letter goes out.
- **Regulatory monitoring**: AI monitors regulatory sources across all relevant jurisdictions daily. Changes mapped to your active matters within 24 hours.

## Slide 5: Your Data — Where It Lives, Who Can See It
- All AI processing happens inside our enterprise infrastructure
- Zero data retention: your documents are never stored by the model provider
- No training on client data: contractual guarantee with all AI vendors
- Row-level security: Client A's data cannot appear in Client B's output
- API-only architecture: no consumer AI tools touch client data

## Slide 6: Governance — Not a Policy Document, Architecture
- Every AI decision is logged, auditable, and replayable
- Full chain of custody: who ran the analysis, which model, what version, what score, whether a human overrode it
- ABA Formal Opinion 512 compliant
- SOC 2, ISO 42001 alignment
- When you ask "prove it," the answer is one query away

---

## Slide 7: What We Don't Do
- We never submit AI output without attorney review
- We never use consumer AI tools on client data
- We never train models on your documents
- We never bill you for AI time at standard rates — fees remain reasonable per ABA 512
- We never deploy AI on your matters without your knowledge

---

## Slide 8: Questions You Should Ask Any Firm About AI
1. Do you use consumer AI tools or enterprise AI with zero data retention?
2. Can you prove your AI vendor doesn't train on client data?
3. Can you show me the audit trail for a decision on one of my matters?
4. What's your override rate — how often do attorneys correct the AI's output?
5. How do you track ROI on your AI investments?

We can answer all five. Most firms can't answer #2, #3, or #5.
