# Enablement Kit

The Enablement Kit provides materials for upskilling lawyers and staff in AI literacy, in partnership with Learning & Development. All materials are designed to be actionable without modification.

## What's Included

### Workshop Deck Outline

A 60-minute workshop structure covering:
- What AI can and cannot do in legal practice
- How to write effective prompts with confidentiality boundaries
- Hands-on exercise with real legal scenarios
- Q&A and next steps

### Prompt Engineering for Lawyers

A practical guide covering:
- Structuring prompts for legal analysis (context, role, task, format)
- Confidentiality: what you can and cannot paste into an AI tool
- Examples: good prompts vs risky prompts
- Common failure modes and how to catch them

### Adoption Playbook

A champion-network approach to AI adoption:
- Identifying and recruiting practice group champions
- Lunch-and-learn format for initial exposure
- Feedback loops: how to report issues and suggest improvements
- Measuring adoption: what "using AI" actually means

### AI Literacy FAQ

Common lawyer questions and direct answers:
- "Will this replace me?" — No. It handles the routine so you can focus on judgment.
- "Is my client data safe?" — Here's the architecture that ensures confidentiality.
- "What if the AI is wrong?" — That's why every output has human review gated in.
- "How do I bill for AI-assisted work?" — Per ABA guidance and client-specific agreements.

### Client Conversation Deck Outline

Materials for relationship partners to discuss AI with clients:
- How to position AI as a value-added differentiator
- What clients actually care about (speed, cost, accuracy, confidentiality)
- Sample language for engagement letters and pitch decks
- Governance evidence to share (audit trail, ABA 512 compliance)

### RFP Response Templates

Pre-written responses for common AI-related RFP questions:
- "Describe your firm's use of artificial intelligence."
- "How do you ensure confidentiality when using AI tools?"
- "What governance framework oversees your AI systems?"
- "How do you measure the quality and accuracy of AI outputs?"

### Technical FAQ for Client CISOs

Answers to technical questions client security teams ask:
- Data retention and deletion policies
- Encryption at rest and in transit
- Access controls and audit logging
- Third-party subprocessor relationships
- SOC 2 and ISO 42001 alignment

## Using the Enablement Kit

All materials are available at `/guides/enablement-kit`. Each document is self-contained and can be shared directly with the relevant audience (lawyers, partners, clients, IT).
