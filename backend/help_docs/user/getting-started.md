# Getting Started with Legal AI OS

Legal AI OS is a governed AI platform for legal enterprises. It provides an independent measurement, governance, and enablement layer around your existing AI tools (like Harvey AI).

## What Legal AI OS Does

Legal AI OS operates across four layers:

1. **Program Operations** — Portfolio dashboard, ROI framework (cost impact, quality, adoption), POC pipeline tracker, enablement kit
2. **Governance & Trust** — Audit trail, explainability, traceability, confidentiality (RLS), human-in-the-loop gating, compliance readiness (ABA 512, SOC 2, ISO 42001)
3. **Harvey Agent Monitoring** — Independent 4-dimension evaluation of Harvey outputs (accuracy, safety, bias, compliance), drift detection, certification
4. **AI Functions** — 8 POC demo functions (matter intake, contract review, employment, due diligence, regulatory monitor, KM intelligence, value reporting, AI maturity)

## First Steps

1. **Check the Dashboard** — Navigate to **Dashboard** in the Operations sidebar to see ROI metrics across all functions
2. **Explore the POC Pipeline** — Go to **POC Pipeline** to see active AI projects and their status
3. **Review AI Maturity** — Visit **AI Maturity** under Functions to assess your organization's AI readiness
4. **Set Up Harvey Monitoring** — Register Harvey agents in **Harvey Monitor** to begin independent evaluation

## Key Concepts

- **ROI Framework**: Measures time saved, cost avoided (time saved × billable rate), quality (accuracy, override rate), and adoption (active / eligible users)
- **POC Pipeline**: Tracks AI projects through Discovery → Build → Review → Graduated (or Cancelled)
- **Harvey Evaluation**: 4-dimension scoring (Accuracy, Safety, Bias, Compliance) with weighted scoring and hard veto at 75 on any Tier 1 dimension
- **Governance**: Every decision is auditable, explainable, and traceable — by architecture, not by policy
